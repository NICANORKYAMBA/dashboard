Renewable Energy

Renewable energy is energy that is collected from renewable resources, which are naturally replenished on a human timescale, such as sunlight, wind, rain, tides, waves, and geothermal heat.

Why Renewable Energy?

Renewable energy sources are essential for reducing greenhouse gas emissions and combating climate change. They provide a sustainable and clean alternative to fossil fuels, helping to preserve the environment for future generations.

Investing in renewable energy also promotes energy security, creates jobs, and stimulates economic growth. By harnessing the power of natural resources, we can build a more resilient and sustainable energy system.
